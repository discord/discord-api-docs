We're introducing three new modal components: **Radio Groups**, **Checkbox Groups**, and **Checkboxes**. These components expand the ways users can interact with your app through modals, enabling single-choice selections, multi-select options, and simple yes/no toggles.

#### The New Components

- [**Radio Group**](/docs/components/reference#radio-group): A single-selection component for choosing exactly one option from a list. Supports the `required` field to allow optional selections.
- [**Checkbox Group**](/docs/components/reference#checkbox-group): A multi-selection component with configurable `min_values` and `max_values` constraints for flexible selection requirements.
- [**Checkbox**](/docs/components/reference#checkbox): A simple boolean toggle for yes/no style questions.

All three components must be placed inside a [**Label**](/docs/components/reference#label) component and are only available in modals.
